Solving Problems

Notifications

You might need to enable badges and alerts in Notification Center inside Settings.app on your device.

Completed list

To move tasks to Completed list you need to Clear completed tasks in List. To do that tap Trash icon with checkmark above it on the tasks page.

To restore Task simply uncheck it.
