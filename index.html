How To Use Support

GoTasks

Google Tasks for iPhone and iPad

Features

  • Manage and synchronize tasks with Google or Google Apps account
  • Multiple account support
  • Search
  • Inplace tasks editing
  • Notifications of upcoming tasks
  • Sort manually or by date, by title or by status
  • Badges showing the number of tasks (due, overdue, today)
  • Highlight due / overdue tasks
  • Offline mode. Edit tasks offline and sync them later
  • Data is sent securely over HTTPS to Google
